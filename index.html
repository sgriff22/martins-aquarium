Martin's Aquarium

Tank Maintenance Tips

Daily Maintenance

  • Remove excess water
  • Monitor water level
  • Evaluate fish swimming and appetite
  • Check water temperature

Weekly Maintenance

  • 10% water change with gravel siphon
  • Scrub algae from sides of aquarium
  • Rinse biological filtration gently in reserve tank water
  • Check water quailty

Harvesting Tips From Martin

Harvest fish early in the morning.
Always wet your hands/equipment and keep the fish in the water as long as possible.
Stay away from snapping turtles!
